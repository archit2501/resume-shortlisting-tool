Data Scientist

Company: DataTech Analytics
Location: San Francisco, CA
Employment Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are looking for a talented Data Scientist to join our analytics team. You will be responsible for analyzing large datasets, building predictive models, and providing actionable insights to drive business decisions.

Responsibilities:
• Analyze complex datasets to identify trends and patterns
• Develop and deploy machine learning models
• Create data visualizations and dashboards
• Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business requirements
• Present findings to both technical and non-technical audiences
• Implement statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
• Work with big data technologies and cloud platforms

Required Skills:
• Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
• 2+ years of experience in data science or analytics
• Proficiency in Python and R programming languages
• Experience with machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
• Strong knowledge of statistics and statistical modeling
• Experience with SQL and database management
• Data visualization skills (Tableau, Power BI, matplotlib, seaborn)
• Knowledge of Jupyter notebooks and version control (Git)

Preferred Skills:
• PhD in a quantitative field
• Experience with big data technologies (Hadoop, Spark)
• Cloud platform experience (AWS, Azure, GCP)
• Knowledge of deep learning and neural networks
• Experience with A/B testing and experimental design
• Natural Language Processing (NLP) experience
• Time series analysis and forecasting
• Knowledge of Docker and MLOps practices
